Our Selection Criteria

At Arabfield Academy, our tutors are the heart of our mission. We don't just hire native speakers; we select passionate educators who meet our rigorous standards to ensure you receive the best possible learning experience.

Academic Excellence

All our tutors hold university degrees, with many specializing in Arabic language, literature, or linguistics.

Proven Teaching Experience

We require a minimum of two years of experience specifically in teaching Arabic to non-native speakers.

Exceptional Communication Skills

Our tutors are fluent in English, enabling them to explain complex concepts clearly and build strong rapport.

Passion and Patience

We select individuals who genuinely love teaching and demonstrate immense patience, creating a supportive environment.

Technological Proficiency

Tutors must be adept at using modern online teaching tools to create an engaging virtual classroom.

Cultural & Human Sensitivity

We choose open-minded educators who respect all cultures, fostering an inclusive space where every student feels valued.
